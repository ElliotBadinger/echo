Continuously record audio for retrieval at a later time.

Unless saved, recordings are deleted on a rolling basis.

<ul>
<li>record harassment and bullying (even when you didn't anticipated them)</li>
<li>save important pieces of information (phone numbers / passwords / instructions)</li>
<li>record cute/funny moments</li>
<li>prove that someone said (or didn't say) something</li>
</ul>

<b>Limitations</b>

The app employs a range of tricks to reduce battery usage but it's still draining quite a bit.
You'll have to check your device to see how it affects your battery.

Battery drain is comparable to having constant audio playback.
